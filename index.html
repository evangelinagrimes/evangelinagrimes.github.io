ABOUT VDC

The Valorant Draft Circuit is a free, community-run North American VALORANT league designed around a professional sports draft model. Players join individually, play combines to show their skills, and get drafted onto teams that compete throughout a full season.

This merch drop is made by and for VDC players. Every item is produced in small batches per season — no resellers, no restocks. One drop per season, then it's done.
